# Machine Learning ‚Äì Chapter 3: Classification and Logistic Regression

This project was developed during my work and teaching at **EDHEC Business School** (Nice, France).  
It focuses on **classification algorithms**, particularly **logistic regression**, applied to real-world datasets from Kaggle.  
The exercises explore supervised classification, model evaluation, and interpretation of predictive factors in socio-economic, medical, and quality-assessment contexts.

---

## üìò Overview

The notebook combines theoretical and practical components:

- Binary and multiclass **classification** concepts  
- The mathematics of **logistic regression** and the **cross-entropy loss**  
- Performance evaluation using **accuracy**, **precision**, **recall**, **F1-score**, and **ROC curves**  
- Hands-on implementation of logistic-regression models with real datasets  

Through three case studies‚Äî**Titanic Survival**, **Heart Disease Prediction**, and **Wine Quality Classification**‚Äîthe project demonstrates how data preprocessing, model training, and evaluation connect theory to applied data-driven insights.

- Discuss the potential for preventive diagnostics
